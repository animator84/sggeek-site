Privacy Policy for Diamond Adventure

Effective date: April 15, 2026
Last updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Diamond Adventure mobile application ("the App"), operated by SGGeek, collects, stores, and uses information when you use the App.

Information You Provide

The App lets you create and manage name lists and related content that you enter yourself. That content is sent to and stored on Google Firebase (cloud database / backend services) so the App can save your lists, sync them where that feature is offered, and make them available when you use the App.

Name lists may include names or labels you choose. Do not enter sensitive personal data (for example government ID numbers, full medical information, or passwords) unless you accept the risk of storing it in the App.

Information Collected Automatically

The App and Firebase may also process limited technical data needed to run the service, such as device identifiers, app version, security-related logs, and diagnostic data. Third-party components you enable (for example Google Play Services, analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs) may collect additional data under their own policies.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information above to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App and its features;
  • Store and retrieve the name lists and related content you save in Firebase;
  • Protect the service, prevent abuse, and fix technical issues;
  • Comply with law where required.

If you enable advertising or analytics in a future version of the App, those providers may use data as described in their policies. This page will be updated when that changes.

Service Providers and Subprocessors

We use Google Firebase (and related Google Cloud infrastructure) to host and process data you submit through the App. Google’s handling of data is described in Google’s privacy documentation and the Firebase terms applicable in your region. Please review Google’s privacy policy for how they process data on our behalf.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your stored name lists and related content in Firebase for as long as you keep using the App and the data remains needed to provide the service, unless a longer period is required by law.

You may request deletion of data associated with your use of the App by contacting us at the email below. We will take reasonable steps to delete your stored content where technically feasible, subject to legal retention requirements.

Data Security

We rely on industry-standard practices and Firebase security features to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age required by local law without parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children in a way that violates applicable child-protection rules.

Your Choices and Rights

You can stop sending new data by not entering it in the App, and you can uninstall the App from your device. Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you; contact us using the email below. Some requests may need to be directed to Google where Google processes data solely as a processor.

The App may be offered free of charge while operational costs allow. Hosting, traffic, and related costs can increase as more people use the App.

If we can no longer reasonably offer the current service for free, we may introduce paid plans, subscriptions, usage limits, or other charges, or we may change how features are offered. If we do, we will provide reasonable advance notice when practicable (for example through the App, this privacy page, or the contact email below) before new fees apply, consistent with applicable law and any agreements shown to you in the App or app store.

Continued use of the App after notice may constitute acceptance of the new terms where permitted by law. If you do not agree, you may stop using the App and request deletion of your stored data as described above.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective or last-updated date. Material changes may be communicated through the App or by email where appropriate.
